About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Board Certified Psychiatrist

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Johnson specializes in treating anxiety, depression, ADHD, and trauma-related disorders. She completed her medical training at Johns Hopkins University and her psychiatric residency at UCLA Medical Center.

Dr. Johnson's approach combines evidence-based treatments with a compassionate, holistic perspective. She believes in treating the whole person—not just symptoms—and works collaboratively with each patient to develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and goals.

Our Services

Comprehensive mental health care tailored to your individual needs

Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment to understand your symptoms, medical history, and treatment goals.

  • 90-minute initial consultation
  • Personalized treatment plan

Medication Management

Expert guidance on psychiatric medications, including benefits, risks, and ongoing monitoring.

  • Regular follow-up appointments
  • Medication adjustments as needed

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on addressing specific concerns and developing coping strategies.

  • CBT, DBT, and mindfulness approaches
  • Weekly or biweekly sessions

Stress Management

Learn effective techniques to manage stress and improve your overall well-being.

  • Relaxation techniques
  • Lifestyle modification guidance

Relationship Counseling

Guidance for improving communication and resolving conflicts in relationships.

  • Individual or couples sessions
  • Communication skill building

Trauma Recovery

Specialized treatment for trauma-related disorders using evidence-based approaches.

  • EMDR therapy
  • Trauma-focused CBT
